Spamhaus: Spamhaus is a leading provider of real-time threat intelligence and spam filtering solutions. It maintains various blacklists, including the Spamhaus Block List (SBL), which identifies IP addresses of known spammers, and the Exploits Block List (XBL), which identifies compromised machines. Spamhaus is highly respected for its comprehensive databases and accurate threat intelligence.z

Barracuda Reputation Block List (BRBL): BRBL is a reputation-based blacklist maintained by Barracuda Networks. It helps organizations protect their networks from email-borne threats by identifying IP addresses known for sending spam or malicious content. BRBL integrates seamlessly with Barracuda's email security solutions and is trusted by many organizations for its effectiveness in blocking threats.

VirusTotal: VirusTotal is a free online service that analyzes files and URLs for potential threats. It aggregates data from various antivirus engines and other sources to provide comprehensive threat intelligence. While VirusTotal is primarily known for analyzing files and URLs, it also offers information about blacklisted IPs and domains, making it a valuable tool for cybersecurity professionals.
