White
Hex #FFFFFF
RGB 255, 255, 255
CMYK 0, 0, 0, 0		 This is your standard, run of the mill white. Known to be crisp and pure, this color is often thought of when winter is about to roll around. Another great example is your standard sheet of printer paper or white sketchbook page.
